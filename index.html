About US

Developer Student Clubs or DSC is a program started by google to train individuals around the world to learn about a wide range of technical topics and gain new skills though hands-on workshops, in person training and project building activities. DSC VSSUT was primarily set up to help students advance their skills,career and network. We are a team of passionate individuals who share a common objective of finding cutting edge solutions to day-to-day problems. We are eager to try new ideas and are dedicated to provide a friendly environment for students to grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning atmosphere and collaborate to build impactful solutions.
